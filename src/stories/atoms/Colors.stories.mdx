# Colors
The library's color palette is designed to meet [WCAG 2 Guidelines](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG20/#visual-audio-contrast-contrast) for [minimum color contrast](https://dequeuniversity.com/rules/axe/4.4/color-contrast?application=axeAPI) to font color:

| Theme | Color | Aa - Large Text | Aa - Normal Text | Contrast Ratio |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Light | `turquoise-100` | Black Text - min 49% opacity | Black Text - min 65% opacity | [8.05:1](https://webaim.org/resources/contrastchecker/?fcolor=000000&bcolor=00B0C9) |
| Light | `gold-100` | Black Text - min 46% opacity | Black Text - min 60% opacity | [10.49:1](https://webaim.org/resources/contrastchecker/?fcolor=000000&bcolor=FFA300) |
| Dark | `turquoise-100` | White Text - min 69% opacity | White Text - min 98% opacity | [4.62:1](https://webaim.org/resources/contrastchecker/?fcolor=FFFFFF&bcolor=008098) |
| Dark | `gold-100` | White Text - min 69% opacity | White Text - min 99% opacity | [4.58:1](https://webaim.org/resources/contrastchecker/?fcolor=FFFFFF&bcolor=A56800) |
____
